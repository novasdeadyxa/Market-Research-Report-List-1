<p><p>Continuous manufacturing encompasses various market types that optimize production processes across industries. Semi-continuous systems operate intermittently yet maintain a flow, typically involving milling and weighing equipment. Continuous granulators ensure consistent particle size, while continuous coaters apply coatings uniformly. Continuous blenders mix materials seamlessly, and continuous dryers facilitate moisture removal efficiently. Continuous compressors provide consistent gas pressure, supporting various applications. Each system enhances productivity and reduces cycle times in manufacturing.</p></p>
